What should I do if my internet is down?

First, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Restart your router (power off for 30 seconds then on)
  2. Check all cable connections
  3. Test with a different device
  4. Check our network status page

If the issue persists, contact our support team.

How can I optimize my WiFi connection?

For best WiFi performance:

  • Place your router in a central location
  • Elevate the router (not on the floor)
  • Keep away from metal objects and appliances
  • Use 5GHz band for less interference (if available)
  • Consider our WiFi extender service for large homes
